FAQs
  • What costs apply when changing a booking?
  • To change your booking details you pay a rebooking fee and a handling charge, depending on the type of ticket. If you rebook in a higher booking class you will also have to pay the difference between the original fare and the new one. As a general rule, you may rebook your ticket in the same booking class or a higher one â€“ provided there are still seats available.
  • What could be the problem if web check-in doesn't work for me?
  • Please check the data you have entered for any typing errors, and try again. If there are problems when checking in with your Miles and More number, please insert your ticketnumber or bookingcode instead.
  • How long before departure can I check in online
  • You can check in online as much as 47 hours before departure.
  • Do I receive a booking acknowledgement after completing a booking?
  • After completing the booking you will see a booking confirmation on your screen and a confirmation has been sent to you by e-mail.
